Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Ofivio

1. What is Ofivio and who is it for?

Ofivio is a project management platform designed for architects and construction teams. It unites design workflows, procurement, and construction tracking.

2. How does client approval work?

Clients receive notifications, view progress, comment, approve or object to milestones. Approval is recorded in the project timeline.

3. Can I import BOQs and invoices?

Yes — import CSV/Excel BOQs and attach invoices to procurement items. Integration with accounting systems is available for Enterprise plans.

4. Does Ofivio support multiple currencies?

Yes — set project currency in settings. Enterprise accounts can enforce currency rules by region.

5. How is user access controlled?

Role-based access (Admin, Manager, Designer, Supplier, Client) with fine-grained permissions for projects and procurement.

6. Are my files secure?

Files are stored with encrypted storage and optional enterprise key management for compliance plans.

7. What integrations are available?

Popular integrations include Google Drive, GitHub, Slack, and accounting software. More available for Enterprise accounts.

8. Do you provide onboarding?

Yes — paid onboarding and training packages are available for medium and enterprise customers.

9. Can I host Ofivio on my own infra?

We offer a cloud SaaS and an on-premise option for large enterprise customers with compliance needs.

10. How do I get support?

Email support@ofivio.com or use the in-app chat. Enterprise customers have 24/7 priority support.
